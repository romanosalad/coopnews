# CoopNews Engine V1.0 Guidelines

CoopNews is not an institutional news portal. It is a market intelligence product for the cooperative ecosystem.

## Editorial Identity

- Reference: B9 and Mundo do Marketing.
- Visual vibe: clean layout, strong typography, high contrast, off-white/black base, lime and orange accents.
- Voice: analytical, provocative and strategic.
- Lens: market psychoanalysis, community branding, concrete value.
- Mantra: concrete value above everything.

## Content Desks

| Desk | Primary focus | Secondary focus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Capa | Advertising campaigns and major marketing moves. | Cooperative history and executive moves. |
| CoopTech | AI and automation translated into cooperative value. | National and international AI implementation cases. |
| La Fora | B Corp, ESG and good marketing cases that inspire cooperatives. | Global martech and consumer trends. |
| Vozes | Human opinion from professionals. | Technical debates about the future of the sector. |
| Forum | Community relevance ranking. | Technical discussion and comments. |

## C-MAD Intelligence Matrix

Every AI-refined article must answer:

- C, Coop Business: how does this create prosperity for members or solve a cooperative-model pain?
- M, Marketing: what is the branding strategy, awareness level or market positioning?
- A, Art/Craft: what can be read in art direction, photography, copy and originality?
- D, Design/UX: how does it improve the member journey and belonging?

## AI Editorial Rules

- Rewrite in Brazilian Portuguese. Do not translate literally.
- Do not copy long passages from the original source.
- Open with the fact, then add context, strategic interpretation and impact.
- Keep cover titles under 75 characters when possible.
- Keep leads under 160 characters in the feed.
- Use H2 sections in long articles every roughly 300 words.
- Preserve the original source URL and show it at the end of the article.
- Prefer the original `og:image` from the source article.
- Discard generic SEO explainers, weak roundups and articles where cooperativism is only inferred.

## Publishing Standard

Autonomous publishing is allowed only when:

- The source explicitly mentions a cooperative, co-op, credit union, mutual, building society or cooperative brand.
- The article is relevant to marketing, advertising, branding, martech, communications, customer/member experience or community impact.
- The AI returns a complete rewritten article, 5 story slides and relevance score above the configured threshold.
